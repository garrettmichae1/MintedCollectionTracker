1. Privacy Policy

Minted is designed with a "Privacy First" architecture. We believe your inventory data belongs to you.

Data Storage

All data (collections, items, photos, and values) is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. If you have iCloud enabled, your data syncs privately to your personal iCloud container. We (the developers) cannot access, view, or sell your inventory data.

Analytics

We do not use third-party tracking services. Your usage habits remain private.

2. Terms of Service

By utilizing Minted: Collection Tracker, you agree to the following terms.

The Collector's Pass (IAP)

The "Collector's Pass" is a one-time, non-consumable In-App Purchase. It unlocks unlimited item storage and unlimited collections. Payments are processed securely by Apple. Because this is a one-time purchase, it does not expire.

Refunds

All payment processing and refund requests are handled directly by Apple Support. We do not have the ability to issue refunds manually.
